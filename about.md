# Integrated TPL

A simple Geode mod that integrates **[The Piss List](https://www.thepisslist.com/)** into Geometry Dash, allowing you to view rankings directly in-game.

> **Note:** This mod is a fork of [Integrated Demonlist](https://github.com/hiimjasmine00/IntegratedDemonlist) by hiimjasmine00.

## Features
- **List Browser:** A new button in the search menu to browse the full challenge list.
- **Search:** Easily search for specific challenges by name.
